About HDO Pak

A Journey of Resilience
& Community Action.

Established in 2005, we have evolved from a small community-based organization into a robust regional force for good. We work where the need is greatest, often in the most underserved districts of Balochistan.

Mission Driven

Empowering youth in decision-making and service delivery.

Core Values

Equality, Self-Reliance, and Peace guide every program we run.

Our Track Record

Proven Field Impact

Emergency Relief

Responded to the floods in Jaffarabad, serving 2,000+ families with food and medical aid via 15 relief camps.

Digital Security

Empowering youth and community members with essential technical and security training for the modern digital age.

Literacy Centers

Helped 150+ adults gain essential reading skills and built 12 temporary learning centers for children.
